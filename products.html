Showcolate Products

Showcolate’s kiosks are fully-equipped to serve our delicious products consisting of selected fresh fruits and the finest chocolates. Ranging from chocolate covered strawberry skewers to a multi-fruit fondue covered with whipped cream and nuts, Showcolate has mastered the art of delicacy.


Sensation Fondue

Selected fruit in a delicious fondue that will entice your senses!


Passion Fondue

A mesmerizing mixture of chocolate, strawberries, whipped cream and nuts that will have chocolate lovers coming back for more!


Twocolate

An exciting mixture of milk and white chocolates, fresh fruits and chocolate chips that transforms fondue into fun!


Fruit Skewers

A mouthwatering selection of fruit served on a skewer and covered in dark, milk or white chocolate!


Strawberry Dipped

A delicious prime strawberry dipped with the finest chocolate!


Party Trays

Convenient and classy, our party trays are perfect for holidays, dinners and special occasions.


But that’s not all; Showcolate is constantly creating recipes and adding easy on-the-go desserts to its product line to keep up with market demand.
